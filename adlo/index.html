Why Adlo uses Google access

Adlo uses Gmail read-only access to identify expense-related emails like receipts, order confirmations, payment confirmations, refunds, bookings, and subscription notices. It uses that information to help organize your spending inside the app. Adlo does not send email, sell your data, or use Gmail data for advertising.

Monitor recurring items

Follow repurchase timing, price changes, and likely restock windows.

Generate insights

See trend, projection, and recurring-purchase insights based on your own expense activity.
